Learn to Engage Mindfully 🧘

🌱 Emotional intelligence helps you understand and express your feelings better — and connect more meaningfully with others.

💬 How to Express Your Mood in Conversation

  • “I'm feeling a bit low today. I think I just need some quiet time.”
  • “I’m so happy! I wanted to share this good news with you.”
  • “I’m feeling tense and I need a break to cool off.”
  • “I’m not sure how I feel yet, but I wanted to talk anyway.”

👂 Mindful Listening Tips

  • Put your phone away and face the person.
  • Don’t interrupt — let them finish speaking.
  • Repeat back what you heard to show understanding.
  • Use calm body language and soft tone.

🧘 Mindful Communication Habits

  • Pause before responding — take a breath.
  • Speak from your own feelings, not assumptions.
  • Ask: “How can I support you right now?”
  • Say “thank you for sharing that” often.

By practicing mindful expression and listening, we create safer spaces for each other — and healthier relationships. 💖
